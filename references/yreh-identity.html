Brand principles — 03
01

Small bets, fast loops

Launch in weeks, not months. Every product is a hypothesis — the market is the only judge that matters.

02

Revenue is signal

Downloads and stars are vanity. Someone paying you money is the only proof that you've solved a real problem.

03

Kill fast, double down slow

Most projects should die young. When one gets traction, that's when patience and craft kick in.

04

Stay default alive

No fundraising. No burn rate. Build from revenue, keep costs near zero, and own every decision.
